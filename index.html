About Us

We are passionate about revolutionizing the way people navigate indoor spaces

We Are Navigators

We are a team of dedicated tech enthusiasts, architects, engineers, and designers who came together with a common goal: to eliminate the confusion that often comes with navigating indoors. With a deep understanding of cutting-edge technology and a commitment to enhancing user experiences, we have developed a state-of-the-art indoor navigation platform that combines accuracy, simplicity, and innovation.

We provide a seamless indoor navigation experience using advanced technologies such as Bluetooth beacons, Wi-Fi positioning, and augmented reality. Our user-friendly mobile app and web platform help users find the shortest routes, locate specific stores, offices, amenities, and much more within large indoor spaces. We believe that everyone should have access to convenient and reliable indoor navigation, and we strive to make it possible through our intuitive solutions.
